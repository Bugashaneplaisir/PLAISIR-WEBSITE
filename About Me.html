Bugashane is a boy who was born in D.R.Congo from a family where they were born nine(9) children, 4 boys and 5 girls. He was the fifth born of the family and he was born in October 1985 in a village named Ndolera in the Democratic Republic of Congo.His father is by the name MASONGA RUKWEGA, son of KISHWIRA KAJUNGA who was married to three wives: LAMINANI, NGOYERA MAKUYANO and KIJIBINGI,his mother is MAUWA MUBYALIRO, daughter of NAMUSHEGO AND SIKUZANI. Bugashane started his primary school back in D.R.Congo in 1992 at E.P SHIBINDA reaching in class 3 he went to E.P LUVUNGI where he completed his primary school and have been awarded a primary school certificate,obtened with 57%. then he undertook the Secondary school at Innstitut Itara, a Catholic Convetional school in 2001 to 2007, he presented the national examination but was not successful, he left the school and oriennted himself in Computer World where he did 6 months training and has been awarded a certificate in 2010. afterward he stated working in a Public Secretariat from 2010 to 2011 in March when he joined the CENI team for the enrollement of citizens issuing them voting cards, and that work was completed in July, then he went back to his normal activities. in 2012 september he registered himself once more in form six to look for a document testifying the completion of the secondary school, and in 2013 August he got his Diplome d'Etat with 62%.
